### Add Gaussian vertex attribute

This is a depricated operation! Please use <<add-random-vertex-attribute>> instead.

Generates a new random double attribute with a Gaussian (normal) distribution
with a mean of 0 and a standard deviation of 1.

====
[[name]] Attribute name::
The new attribute will be created under this name.

[[seed]] Seed::
The random seed.
